Development


Check out a couple of web niknak's I've designed and developed. First up an iOS and Android app SafeTraveller, then my personal favourite web creation working at Inspyre Design, `The Galaxy`.

SafeTraveller is an iOS and Android app developed as an aid for hitchhikers and the solo traveller.
As the sole developer for the SafeTraveller SaaS, I was able to experience all facets of the app creation lifecycle from design to ship - creating the UI, learning and working with new frameworks and technologies on the fly, creating promotional material for marketing, transitioning into an app store production model, and much more.
Alongside the mobile app I developed a web portal providing user trip management for SafeTraveller. SafeTraveller technologies utilised include Kendo MVVM UI Framework, Apache Cordova (background geolocation, push notifications, in app purchase), Telerik Everlive MBaaS, RESTful Emergency Contact API's.
